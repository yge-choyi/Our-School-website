Background history

HMBS is a boarding school found in 2010 EC. by the joint work of gogota care, a non-governmental organization, and the best international scholars of our community who strive to change the poor conditions of education in our locality. since then our school is a model for many schools in our region. It offered a better learning environment to our students.

The Center of Excellence

We strive to be a center of excellence in education. At our school, we are committed to providing the highest quality education to all our students. We believe that every student has the potential to succeed and we are here to help them reach their full potential. Being a center of excellence means that we are dedicated to excellence in all areas of education. We are constantly striving to improve our teaching methods, curriculum, and facilities to ensure that our students receive the best possible education. Our teachers are highly qualified and experienced, and they are committed to creating a learning environment that is both challenging and supportive. They use a variety of teaching methods to cater to the diverse learning styles of our students, and they are always available to provide extra help and support when needed. We also have a strong focus on extracurricular activities, such as sports, music, drama, and art. We believe that these activities are an important part of a well-rounded education, and they help to develop important skills such as teamwork, leadership, and creativity. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our academic achievements. Our students consistently perform well in national and international exams, and many of our graduates go on to attend some of the world's top universities. We also have a strong partnership with the local community and industry, which allows us to provide our students with real-world learning experiences and opportunities to develop important skills that will prepare them for the workforce. In summary, being a center of excellence means that we are committed to providing the highest quality education to our students. We are constantly striving to improve and innovate in all areas of education, and we believe that our students have the potential to achieve great things. If you are looking for a school that is dedicated to excellence in education, then we invite you to join us and be a part of our community.
